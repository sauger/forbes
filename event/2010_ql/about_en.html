About 2009 Forbes China Best SME List
　　Forbes China Best SME List started ranking from 2005 and aims to help China local SME step to world stage and present China local enterprises development status and trend to China economic exports and both China & foreign investors. The research covers over 300,000 SME enterprises across China from sector to sector.
Eligibility of Candidate Enterprise/span>
·Non-state-owned enterprise operating its main businesses in Mainland China
·Enterprise entity with independent operation and accounting; not branch or subsidiary; not partner of enterprise; bank, franchisee and utilities company are not eligible
·Revenue of the latest year falls within the range of RMB50-1,000 million
·Established before Jan. 1, 2006 and with at least 3 years’ complete operating history and record (for the purpose of covering as many vibrant and promising enterprises as possible, while ensuring stability in corporate performance)
·Research covers nearly all industries, including service, high-tech, media and entertainment rather than confined to the traditional manufacturing industry
Method of Research and Ranking
·Method of Research: date research combined with on-site visit. We interview the enterprises after analyzing their financial data.
·Method of Ranking: the ranking of enterprises is determined by calculating the weighted aggregate of growth indices (sales growth and profit growth rates), ROI indices (ROI and ROE) and profit index (profit rate) as well as sales revenue in the past three years.
　About Forbes China Midas List
　　Forbes China Midas list is the annual ranking by Forbes China Magazine of the best venture capitalists. The name is an allusion to the mythological King Midas, renowned for his ability to turn anything he touched into gold.
　　In order to promote the development of China’s venture capital industry, and provide reference for start-up company who is looking for investors, Forbes China makes the Midas list which aims to release the best investors, who have unique vision, be good at seizing opportunities in China market and help start-up companies growing up.
Method of Research and Ranking
·Method of Research: desk research, questionnaire survey, and interview.
·Research Object: venture capitalists who Invest in China market and having exited project in the past 5 years （2004.7.1-2009.6.30）
·Method of Ranking: performance of the exited company and how much he/she contributes to the project.
